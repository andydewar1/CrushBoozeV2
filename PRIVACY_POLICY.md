# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 24, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to CrushNic ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our CrushNic mobile application (the "App") and website at [crushnic.com](https://crushnic.com).

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:**
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- Password (encrypted and stored securely)
- Profile preferences and settings

**Quit Journey Data:**
- Quit date and quit status
- Personal motivation statements and reasons for quitting
- Selected personal goals (health, financial, lifestyle)
- Vaping product information (types, quantities, costs)
- Daily vaping costs and financial calculations

**Goal and Progress Tracking:**
- Financial goals and target amounts
- Personal achievement goals
- Goal descriptions and progress tracking
- Money saved calculations and statistics

**Craving and Log Data:**
- Craving intensity levels (1-10 scale)
- Trigger identification and descriptions
- Coping strategy notes and effectiveness
- Timestamps and session data
- Personal notes and reflections

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Analytics:**
- App usage patterns and session duration
- Feature usage statistics and interaction data
- Navigation patterns within the app
- Achievement unlock patterns and milestone tracking

**Device Information:**
- Device type, model, and operating system version
- App version and technical specifications
- Crash reports and error logs (anonymized)
- Performance metrics and optimization data

**Security and Authentication:**
- Login timestamps and session information
- Authentication token management
- Account security events and access logs

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:
- Precise location data or GPS coordinates
- Contacts, address book, or phone directory information
- Photos, videos, or media files from your device
- Browser history or web browsing data
- Information from other apps on your device
- Social media profiles or external account information
- Biometric data or health records beyond what you explicitly provide
- Third-party advertising identifiers

- Data redundancy and high availability architecture

**Encryption Standards:**
- All data encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 protocols

- Authentication tokens secured with industry-standard methods
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data transmissions

### Security Measures

**Access Controls:**
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- Role-based access controls for development team
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Automated monitoring for suspicious activities

**Data Protection:**
- Regular security updates and vulnerability assessments

### Data Retention
